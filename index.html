Welcome to Papaya Jam.com, your go-to destination for delicious and refreshing papaya jam! Our jam is made from the freshest papayas, carefully selected to ensure the highest quality and taste. Whether you're spreading it on toast, adding it to desserts, or using it as a topping for yogurt, our papaya jam is sure to delight your taste buds. Experience the sweet and tropical flavor of our homemade papaya jam today!

Ingredients:

1 ripe papaya 500g cup granulated sugar 1 lemon

Step 1: Prepare the Papaya

Cut the papaya in half and remove the seeds

Step 2: Papaya In Container

Using a spoon, scoop the papaya flesh into a container and mash it until smooth.

Step 3: Put in to pan

Transfer the mashed papaya into a saucepan and add sugar and lemon juice.

Step 4: Cook the Jam

Cook the mixture over medium heat, stirring frequently for 30 minutes, until it thickens to a jam-like consistency.

Step 5: Cool and Store

Remove the saucepan from heat and let the jam cool. Once cooled, transfer it to sterilized jars for storage.
